Tableau projects

The final key in a data analyst's journey is data visualization. Colors, shapes, and patterns catch people's attention. Data visualizations ('vizzes") present data findings quickly, effectively, and in an understandable manner.

I use Tableau to integrate data into interactive dashboard visualizations that appeal to my target audience. It is especially useful when dealing with big data, and it works with a wide range of data.
